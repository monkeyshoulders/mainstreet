Maximizing Real Estate Value for over Twenty Years

Mainstreet Capital Partners is real estate investment and management firm specializing in providing value-added returns through acquisition, development and repositioning of office industrial and multi-family properties

About

Started in 1999 by Paul J. Kilgallon, Mainstreet Capital has a proven track record of value creation through the acquisition, development and repositioning of office, industrial and multi- family properties. Acting on behalf of several institutional partners, Mainstreet actively manages its investments through its subsidiary, Mainstreet Real Estate Services, Inc. Through its extensive experience, market knowledge and long-term relationships, along with hands-on management, Mainstreet has created successful repositioning and value enhancing results in over forty transactions, totaling more than $1 billion in investment.

Paul J. Kilgallon

President, Partner

Paul formed Mainstreet in 1999 and overseeing all of the firm’s activities, with an emphasis on Florida and Georgia. Prior to starting Mainstreet, Paul was a partner at CMD Realty Investors overseeing the acquisition and development of office properties in Virginia, North Carolina,Georgia and Florida. He holds a Masters of Management from the J.L. Kellogg Graduate School of Management at Northwestern University, and an B. S. in Civil Engineering from the University of Illinois, Champaign.

Peter B. Tonon, Sr.

Executive Vice President, Partner

Peter joined Mainstreet in 2000 after spending 14 years with Chesterton Binswanger where he was a Managing Director for Investment. There he was responsible for the sale of investment properties in the Southeastern United States, and structuring sale-leasebacks for the firm’s corporate clients. Peter currently is responsible for North and South Carolina where Mainstreet has been involved in almost 3.5 Million square feet of projects, and for managing Mainstreet’s capital relationships. Peter graduated from the George Washington University with a degree in Business Administration.

David Warren

Partner

David leads Mainstreet’s team in the Southwest. Prior to joining Mainstreet in 2001, David was a partner and Western Region Investment Manager at CMD Realty Investors, acquiring four-million square feet in Phoenix, Denver, Colorado Springs, Los Angeles and Seattle. He has successfully acquired and disposed of over 2 million square feet of transactions in Phoenix. David is a native Arizonan, and graduate of Arizona State University.

Joseph A. DiMario

Vice President, Chief Financial Officer

Joe is responsible for facilitating all financing, acquisition and dispositions and all the accounting and financial reporting and risk management functions for the firm. Before joining Mainstreet in 2001, Joe was a Director of Financial Planning and Business Development for Silversea Cruises, Ltd where he was responsible for budgeting, strategic planning and cash management functions. He holds a Master Degree in Business Administration for Florida Atlantic University, and a Bachelors in Business from the University of Notre Dame, Indiana.

Tamar Z. Jacobs

Vice President, Operations

An original member of the staff, Tamar joined Mainstreet in 1999, and is responsible for overseeing the management, leasing and construction management for the firm’s investment portfolio, along with providing due diligence support for all acquisitions and dispositions, and the redevelopment and renovations of the firm’s investments. Prior to joining Mainstreet, Tamar worked for Jones Lang LaSalle. Tamar holds both a Master’s Degree and Bachelor’s Degree from Nova Southeastern University.

Abe Ibrahim

Controller

Abe Ibrahim has served as Controller for Mainstreet since 2007. He oversees the accounting operations of the company including financial reporting, cash management and tax and audit functions.
