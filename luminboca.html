Lumin Boca Apartments

Boca Raton Florida
(Current Investment)

Mainstreet acquired 37 acres in The Park at Broken Sound in 2007, comprised of a 5-story Class A office building and two single-story industrial buildings. The project was recapitalized in 2015, with the intention of demolishing the industrial buildings to create a higher use for the land, following the approval of the Planned Mobility District Zoning Overlay. The Lumin was developed as a 180-unit Class AA multi-family project that was completed in 2018.
